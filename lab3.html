What is Lemon Tea?

Lemon Tea is a simple and easy drink to make. It's refreshing, soothing, and is healthy for you!

Ingredients

  • Black or Green Tea
  • Water
  • Lemon
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil.
  2. Steep tea for 1 minute.
  3. Strain tea and serve in cup.
  4. Add sliced lemons or lemon juice, and honey. Stir until mixed.
